Let's begin with the Pasta

Shopping list

  • Pasta
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Parmeggiano
  • Basil

Guide

  1. Boil the water & add salt to the water
  2. Cook the pasta for 4-5 minutes al dente
  3. Get a frying pan and quickly bake the pasta, add a little bit of the pasta water and finish with salt & Pepper
  4. Plate up, add the Parmeggiano and basil & bon appetit
